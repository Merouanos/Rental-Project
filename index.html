Privacy & SMS Policy

Information Use

Information collected via this website is used solely for rental coordination purposes. We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing campaigns, and we do not sell, rent, or lease contact information to any third party.

Data collected includes name, phone number, and optional email address, processed only in connection with your coordination request and retained in accordance with applicable legal requirements.

SMS & Carrier Compliance

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Our SMS program is registered with The Campaign Registry (TCR) and compliant with all carrier guidelines and TCPA requirements. Opt-in is voluntary and may be revoked at any time by replying STOP.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the number listed on this page.

Opt-out requests via STOP are processed immediately. No further SMS messages will be sent after an opt-out, except for a single confirmation. You may re-subscribe at any time by replying START.

By submitting a coordination request, you acknowledge you have read and agree to this Privacy & SMS Policy. This policy may be updated periodically; continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
